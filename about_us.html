About Epic Youth Organization-Kenya

EMPOWERING YOUTH IN KENYAN COMMUNITIES

Epic Youth Organization-Kenya (EYO) is dedicated to the holistic empowerment of young people across Kenya. We focus on protecting health, education, livelihood, economic empowerment, and policy advocacy related to human rights for all, including young people.

Since our inception in 2013 and official registration in 2020, EYO has employed a holistic approach to youth empowerment through capacity strengthening, community engagement, health financing, media involvement, entrepreneurship incubation, and policy advocacy. Our mission is to build resilient communities by empowering the youth with the skills and resources they need to thrive.
