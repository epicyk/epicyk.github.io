About Epic Youth Organization-Kenya

Epic Youth Organization-Kenya (EYO) is a nationally recognized non-profit and non-political community-based organization dedicated to empowering young people across Kenya. Officially registered under the Ministry of East Africa Community, Labour & Social Protection, we are headquartered in Mombasa, Kenya.

Our mission is to protect health, education, livelihood, and economic empowerment, and to advocate for policy changes related to human rights for all, including young people. We believe in harnessing the potential of youth to drive sustainable development and create a brighter future for Kenya.

Founded in 2013, EYO began as a small cohort of driven young minds focused on tackling youth challenges through lobbying and community theatre. Today, we have grown into a beacon of hope, continuously expanding our reach and impact. We employ a holistic approach to youth empowerment, focusing on capacity strengthening, community engagement, health financing, media involvement, livelihoods, entrepreneurship incubation, and policy advocacy.
