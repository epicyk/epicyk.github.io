About Epic Youth Organization-Kenya

EMPOWERING YOUTH IN KENYAN COMMUNITIES

Epic Youth Organization-Kenya (EYO) is dedicated to the holistic empowerment of young people across Kenya. We focus on protecting health, education, livelihood, economic empowerment, and policy advocacy related to human rights for all, including young people.

Since our inception in 2013 and official registration in 2020, EYO has employed a holistic approach to youth empowerment through capacity strengthening, community engagement, health financing, media involvement, entrepreneurship incubation, and policy advocacy. Our mission is to build resilient communities by empowering the youth with the skills and resources they need to thrive.

OUR IMPACT

Epic Youth Organization-Kenya is dedicated to creating positive change in communities across Kenya. Through our various programs, we are empowering youth, improving education, and promoting health and wellness. Our efforts have led to tangible outcomes, including:

  • Improved Education: We have built and renovated schools, provided educational materials, and supported teacher training programs to ensure quality education for all.
  • Health and Wellness: Our health initiatives, such as free health screenings and community health fairs, have improved access to essential health services and raised awareness about healthy living.
  • Economic Empowerment: By offering vocational training and entrepreneurship incubation, we have helped young people develop skills and start their own businesses, contributing to economic growth.
  • Community Engagement: Through various community engagement projects, we have fostered a sense of unity and collaboration, encouraging active participation in community development.

Our mission is to continue making a difference by addressing the needs of our communities and empowering the next generation to lead and inspire positive change.
